Welcome to SCP

Explore the world of anomalous entities, objects, and phenomena meticulously cataloged and contained for global safety. Our secure facilities house the SCPs—strange and inexplicable objects—each with their own unique and often dangerous properties. As a Foundation researcher, you are granted access to classified information about these entities, as well as the latest containment protocols and research updates. Browse through our database to uncover the mysteries and ensure the protection of humanity from the unknown.

Catalogue

SCP-002

Containment Class: Euclid



Resembling a fleshy tumour roughly the volume of 60m³. The interior forms an apartment, filled with biological furnishings. This bio-matter consists of human substances.

SCP-003

Containment Class: Euclid



SCP-003 comes in two parts; SCP-003-1 an unknown circuit board, materialised with chitin and an unknown biological sunstance. SCP-003-2, is the stone slab housing this circuit board.

SCP-004

Containment Class: Euclid



SCP-004 is made up of a wooden door leading to an abandonded factory, and a set of 12 rusty keys

SCP-005

Containment Class: Safe



An ornate key with the unique ability of openeing any lock it comes across, digital or physical.

SCP-006

Containment Class: Safe



A spring arousing no real suspicion, except that it's mineral water bears the qualities of 'health'.
